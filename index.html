ABOUT VIIRAT CCONSTRUCTION

Our Legacy: Viirat CConstruction, an extension of the esteemed Charotar Construction Co(Contractor & Engineering), embodies a legacy of 65 years in the industry. We specialize in a diverse range of construction services including new constructions, repair, rehabilitation, waterproofing, and industrial projects.

Our Expertise: Our clientele includes building owners and multinational corporations. We pride ourselves on our green and golden aesthetic, delivering projects that combine functionality with visual appeal.

Our Commitment: As your reliable partner in construction and renovation projects, we are committed to delivering top-notch quality and excellence. Our skilled team is devoted to offering customized solutions that cater to your specific construction needs.

Our Services: We excel in Architect, new builds, Electrification, Fabrications, Home Automation, Light Studios, effective waterproofing services, and expanding existing structures. Our comprehensive range of services includes epoxy applications, injection grouting, painting, and floor coatings.

Our Vision: Enter a realm of dependability and craftsmanship with Viirat Construction, where your aspirations become our priority. We strive to transform your construction dreams into reality with precision and dedication.
